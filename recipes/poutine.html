Real Canadian Poutine

Description

A delicious Canadian delight, made to enjoy with fresh cheese curds and creamy gravy. Make sure to serve this coming winter with family and friends!

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 ounce) package of frozen French fries
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1.5 cups beef broth
  • 1.5 cups cheese curds
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 F and grease baking sheet
  2. spread fries in single layer
  3. Bake until light brown (15 minutes)
  4. Melt butter in saucepan over medium-high heat. Whisk in flour vigorously. When bubbles form, set heat to low. Keep stirring until it thins. Whisk in beef broth. Bring to a boil then simmer, stirring occasionally until it thickens (approximately 5 minutes). Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Distribute cheese curds over fries, pour hot gravy over, enjoy! :3
